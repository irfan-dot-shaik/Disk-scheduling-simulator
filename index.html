About This Simulator

This Advanced Disk Scheduling Simulator visualizes four classic disk-scheduling algorithms used in operating systems:

  • FCFS — First-Come First-Served
  • SSTF — Shortest Seek Time First
  • SCAN — Elevator Algorithm
  • C-SCAN — Circular SCAN

Enter your disk requests, choose an algorithm, and watch the disk head move in real-time. Compare all algorithms side-by-side and export results as CSV.
